KYC Privacy Check ?KYC Privacy CheckCheck if your Bitcoin withdrawal from a KYC exchange (Coinbase, Kraken, etc.) can be traced to your current holdings. This simulates what an adversary who knows your exchange withdrawal could discover about where your funds are now.
Cluster Detection ?Cluster DetectionDetects if your Bitcoin addresses are linked together using Common Input Ownership Heuristic (CIOH). When you spend from multiple addresses in one transaction, they become linked and can be attributed to the same owner.
Exchange Proximity ?Exchange ProximityMeasures how many transaction "hops" separate your address from known exchange addresses (Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, etc.). Addresses close to exchanges are easily KYC-linked by chain analysis firms.
UTXO Privacy Rating ?UTXO Privacy RatingAnalyzes each UTXO (unspent coin) at an address and rates its privacy level as Red (high risk), Yellow (medium risk), or Green (low risk). Helps you understand which coins to use carefully.
Transaction Analysis ?Transaction AnalysisExamine any Bitcoin transaction in detail. Enter a TXID to see inputs, outputs, fees, confirmations, and detect potential CoinJoin activity.
